What are NFTs and the Metaverse?

Non - fungible token

NFTs or non-fungible tokens are a new category of digital assets, which are unique, inseparable, and inflexible. They represent ownership of digital and physical assets on the blockchain. From digital artwork to the gaming industry, NFTs are creating a vast impact everywhere.

Metaverse

Metaverse is a digital environment moving on the blockchain, where technologies such as VR and AR can function as visual component providers.
